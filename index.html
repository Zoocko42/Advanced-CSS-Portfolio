About Me

Born in Atlanta, raised in various parts of North Georgia, then having moved right back to Atlanta again, Zac Schneider is the friendly dollar-store version of a Renaissance Man. He's studied theatre with Georgia College and State University, worked at customer service for First Advantage, co-hosts the podcast, "Nerd $hit!", and is now working to be the programmer you need. If you want a caring perfectionist with a flair for the dramatic, Zac Schneider is at your service.
